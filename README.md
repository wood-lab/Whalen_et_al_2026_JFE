# Code associated with Whalen et al. 2026. "Differential impacts of hurricanes on direct and complex life cycle parasites: a 42-year study of riverine fish." Journal of Freshwater Ecology Special Issue on Parasites in Freshwater Ecosystems. https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/02705060.2026.2690282

For a complete description of how these data were collected, please see the corresponding manuscript. We collected and parasitologically dissected over 1000 fish from the Tulane Univeristy Biodiversity Research Institute and, using a BACI design, measured the effects of hurricanes on direct and complex life cycle parasites over a period of 42 years. We found that direct life cycle parasites were negatively impacted by hurricane events. All associated data are presented here.
